Portfolio for Maveli

Who is this guy?

Mahabali(aka 'Maveli'). The noble king and prosperous ruler of naramada area and asura(demon). In hinduism, people included in the group of seven immortals.

Where is gone Mahabali from the earth?

Vamana(Lord Vishnu) stepped and pushed down in the netherworld.

Why do kerala's people celebrate Onam?

In hinduism, after Mahabali gone into netherworld. Lord Vishnu blessed or granted to see the his people on the earth in every year. Because of that, people in kerala welcomes the Mahabali on Onam day.

What is the reason for moving the Mahabali into the netherworld?

Indra deva(Lord of harvest) aka king of devas. Indra deva jealous about the Mahabali for his people were not prays for devas and Mahabali does care about his people with honesty. So that, lord Indra deva feels jealous on the Mahabali. And, Indra deva complained to the Lord Vishnu. Lord Vishnu changed his identity to Vamana for punish and test the Mahabali.
